Begin the Experiment

We will be conducting an experiment to measure the effectiveness of images versus text for user retention. Once you begin, you will be redirected to one of two pages: one text-heavy or one image-heavy. On either of these pages, you will be shown news headlines in either a grid or a list.


You will have a minute to browse the content on the page and we ask you to try, to the best of your ability, to read and absorb as much of the information as you can.


Once time is up, you will be automatically redirected to an exit questionnaire where you will be asked about the headlines you saw.
